Frequently Asked Questions

What is a video timeline? +

A video timeline is a detailed breakdown of your video content, including camera angles, shots, transitions, audio cues, and effects. It serves as a blueprint for your video production.

How do I use the timeline generator? +

Simply describe your video concept in the app, and our AI will generate a detailed timeline with camera angles, shot types, transitions, and audio suggestions. You can then customize each segment.

Can I customize the generated timeline? +

Yes! Our app allows you to edit every aspect of the generated timeline. Adjust camera angles, change shot types, modify transitions, and customize audio settings to match your vision.

What video generation tools do you recommend? +

We recommend Fliki.ai for AI-powered video creation with avatars and voiceovers, and InVideo for professional video editing and production. Both tools integrate seamlessly with timelines created in our generator.

How can I export my timeline? +

You can export your timeline as a detailed document or use it directly in your preferred video editing software. The timeline includes all specifications for camera work, audio, and effects.

Is this tool free to use? +

Yes, the Video Timeline Generator is free to use. We offer premium features for advanced customization and export options. Check the app for details on our pricing plans.

Can I use timelines for commercial projects? +

Absolutely! Timelines created with our generator can be used for commercial video projects. Please review our Terms of Service for specific licensing details and usage rights.
