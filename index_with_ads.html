Frequently Asked Questions

What is a video timeline? +

A video timeline is a detailed breakdown of your video production, showing the sequence of shots, camera angles, audio, and effects timed to specific moments in your video.

Is this tool really free? +

Yes! Video Timeline Generator is completely free to use. We don't charge for any features or require subscriptions.

Can I export my timeline? +

Yes, you can export your timeline in multiple formats including PDF, JSON, and CSV for use in your video editing software.

What video editing software does this work with? +

Our timelines work with all major video editing software including Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and more.

Do I need to create an account? +

No account needed! Just open the tool and start creating. Your timelines are saved locally on your device.

Can I share my timeline with others? +

Yes! You can export your timeline and share it with team members, or use the sharing feature to generate a shareable link.
