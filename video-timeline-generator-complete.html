Frequently Asked Questions

What is a video timeline? +

A video timeline is a detailed breakdown of your video project showing the exact timing, camera angles, shots, audio, effects, and transitions for each scene. It serves as a blueprint for video creators and AI video generation tools.

How do I use the timeline generator? +

Simply describe your video concept, and our generator will create a detailed timeline with specific timings, camera angles, shot types, audio descriptions, and effects. You can then customize each element to match your vision perfectly.

Can I customize the generated timeline? +

Absolutely! You have complete control over every aspect of the timeline. Edit timing, camera angles, shot descriptions, audio cues, effects, transitions, and any other element to perfectly match your creative vision.

What video generation tools do you recommend? +

We recommend Fliki.AI and InVideo as top-tier video generation platforms that work perfectly with our timeline prompts. Both offer AI-powered video creation, professional templates, and easy-to-use interfaces. Check out our recommended tools section for more options.

How can I export my timeline? +

You can copy your timeline to the clipboard with one click, or download it as a text file. Simply use the export buttons in the app to download your timeline and use it with any video generation tool.

Is this tool free to use? +

Yes! The Video Timeline Generator is completely free to use. We offer unlimited timeline generation with no registration required. We earn through affiliate partnerships with video creation tools.

Can I use timelines for commercial projects? +

Yes! Timelines generated by our tool can be used for any purpose, including commercial projects. There are no restrictions on how you use your generated timelines.
